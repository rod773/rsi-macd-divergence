=== RSI‑MACD Divergence (Regular & Hidden) ===

Input Parameters:
  fast   : 12  (default)
  slow   : 26  (default)
  smooth : 9   (default)

Output Columns (added to the OHLC dataframe):
  macd          – fast SMA – slow SMA
  signal        – EMA of macd (smooth periods)
  hist          – macd – signal
  fractal_top   – 5‑bar pivot highs on hist
  fractal_bot   – 5‑bar pivot lows on hist
  regular_bearish – bearish reversal
  hidden_bearish  – bearish continuation
  regular_bullish – bullish reversal
  hidden_bullish  – bullish continuation

=== Strategy Rules ===
- Long when a Regular Bullish (or – with --hidden – Hidden Bullish) divergence appears.
- Short when a Regular Bearish (or – with --hidden – Hidden Bearish) divergence appears.
- Exit the position when:
    • The opposite signal occurs,
    • Take‑Profit (TP) is hit,
    • Stop‑Loss (SL) is hit,
    • Or the data ends.
